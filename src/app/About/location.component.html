About MCAS

Malabar College of Arts and Science affiliated to Calicut University, started as self financing college under Calicut University in 2010. MCAS is the glorious outcome of the noble endeavors Mr. V.K Moidu Haji and Mr. P.K.Ahammed who started it in the year 2010. Under their managing Trusteeship, the college is managed by a registered charitable body, the Malabar Educational Trust, Moodadi. The College by virtue of its charisma has attracted students across the length and breadth of our State.With open arms we welcome you to the fold of MCAS! Join Us!! We shall grow together amidst the glow of Academic Excellence!!!
